# 🚛 EcoFleet – Collaborative AI Logistics Ecosystem

> **An AI-powered collaborative logistics marketplace that connects available truck capacity with shipment demand, inspired by the simplicity of Uber/Ola/Rapido and designed to reduce empty truck miles, transportation costs, and carbon emissions.**

---

## 📌 Overview

EcoFleet is a smart logistics platform where truck owners and businesses can collaborate by sharing unused truck capacity. Instead of trucks returning empty or partially loaded, the platform intelligently matches shipments with available space on existing routes.

The project demonstrates how AI-assisted decision making can improve logistics efficiency while supporting sustainability goals.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

A significant percentage of trucks travel with empty or unused capacity, leading to:

- ⛽ Fuel wastage
- 💸 Higher transportation costs
- 🌍 Increased CO₂ emissions
- 🚚 Low truck utilization
- 🌾 Poor rural logistics connectivity

Traditional logistics platforms optimize operations only within individual companies rather than across an ecosystem.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

EcoFleet creates a collaborative logistics network where:

- Truck owners can publish available truck space
- Users can book transportation for their goods
- AI recommends the best truck based on multiple factors
- Drivers and users can communicate directly
- The platform maximizes truck utilization while reducing empty miles

## 🤖 AI Matching Engine

The AI matching system considers:

- Available truck capacity
- Route similarity
- Remaining truck space
- Estimated efficiency
- Distance from pickup
- Network optimization score

The system then recommends the most suitable trucks for the shipment.
